Beautify music with your hand

Our wireless analog wah-wah has the advantages of a digital control in the form of a wrist strap, breaking with the traditional concept of the pedalboard

Transmitter

The wristband consists of an accelerometer that sends the position signal through bluetooth to the receiver module.

Receiver

This module takes the signal from the musical instrument and modulates it based on the signal it receives from the transmitter.

Enjoy!

Connect the receiver to a speaker and enjoy the music!
